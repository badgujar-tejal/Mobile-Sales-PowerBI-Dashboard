# Mobile Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Project Objective
This interactive Power BI dashboard analyzes mobile phone sales, transaction patterns, and regional performance across major cities. The report tracks key revenue drivers, payment behaviors, and customer sentiment to help stakeholders optimize inventory distribution and targeting strategy.

## Key KPIs Tracked
* **Total Sales:** 769M
* **Total Quantity Sold:** 19K units
* **Total Transactions:** 4K
* **Average Transaction Value:** 40K

## Key Business Insights
1. **Brand Performance:** Apple leads total sales revenue at **161.6M** (783 transactions), closely followed by Samsung at **160M** (775 transactions), OnePlus at **153.7M**, Vivo at **150M**, and Xiaomi at **143.7M**.
2. **Top Performing Models:** Individual high-revenue contributors include the **iPhone SE (60M)**, **OnePlus Nord (58M)**, and **Galaxy Note (56M)**.
3. **Payment Preferences:** Payment methods are highly competitive and distributed evenly. **UPI** leads at **26.25%**, closely trailed by **Credit Cards (25.89%)**, **Cash (25.03%)**, and **Debit Cards (22.83%)**.
4. **Weekly Sales Distribution:** Sales are strong over the weekend and early week, peaking on **Saturday (115M)** and **Monday (114M)**, while sliding to its lowest point on Wednesday (105M).
5. **Customer Ratings:** Product satisfaction skews highly positive, with the **"Good"** category pulling in **496 ratings**, heavily outperforming "Poor" (186) and "Average" (137).

## Technical Power BI Features Used
* **Geographical Mapping:** Integrated an interactive Bing Map visualizer for live geolocation plotting and sales density tracking across major Indian urban hubs.
* **Dynamic Slicers & Filters:** Implemented multi-tier interactive slicing by Month (January - December), Mobile Model, Payment Method, and Brand.
* **Advanced Charting:** Utilized specialized visual layouts, including Funnel Charts for customer ratings, Donut Charts for payment splits, and structured Area-Line graphs for tracking day-wise performance.
* **Data Formatting:** Configured clear KPI blocks displaying smart rounded numbers (M/K format) along with performance comparison buttons (MTD Report / Same Period Last Year).
